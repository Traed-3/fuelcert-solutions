About This Tool

Why This Exists

I've been in the fueling equipment installation and repair industry my entire life — starting when I was 13. My father started his company working on 7-Eleven gas dispensers after his boss was killed in a vehicle accident. I was in 3rd grade. He was adamant about my brother and I working at a young age. We completed our first aboveground tank install the summer before 7th grade.

I created this tool because I know how frustrating it is to prepare for a certification exam without knowing exactly what to focus on. With 30+ years in the fueling equipment industry and hands-on experience with UST installation, repair, and inspection, I personally reviewed every source document to make sure what's actually on the WVDEP exam is in this tool.

This would have saved me a lot of time and money — including the couple of times I failed. I know we would have much rather purchased a subscription to this than paid $450–$800 for a 2 or 3 day course that doesn't even give you the actual questions and answers.

How to Use This Tool

1️⃣
Select your exam track at the top — UST Technician, UST Remover, or UST Inspector. All content filters to that certification.
🃏
Start with Flashcards. These are the best place to begin — quick, focused, and built directly from the exact material on the exam. Flip through them repeatedly until the answers feel automatic.
📝
Move to Practice Quiz once you're comfortable with the flashcards. Questions are drawn randomly from the full pool. You can skip around if needed, and after each answer you'll see the correct answer, explanation, and the exact source reference.
🎯
Take the Practice Exam when you feel ready. It mirrors the real test — 75 questions, 90 minutes, 90% to pass. Keep working at it until you're consistently hitting 90%.
🔍
Use the Document Filter (top right of the tab bar) to drill specific source documents. Know you're solid on PEI RP100-20 but weak on OSHA excavation rules? Filter to just that document and focus there. This is one of the most powerful features in the tool.
🤖
Use the AI Tutor for any question you're unclear on. Ask it anything about the source documents — it's trained on WV Title 33 Series 30, 40 CFR 280, PEI RP100-20, PEI RP1200-19, API RP 1604, API RP 1615, OSHA standards, and all manufacturer manuals.
💡 Pro tip: This tool is the only thing you need to give new hires to get them certified. It replaces those multi-day courses that don't even give you the actual questions — and costs a fraction of the price.

Source Documents Covered

Class A — Installer: WV Title 33 Series 30, 40 CFR 280, PEI RP100-20, API RP 1615, API RP 1632, OSHA 1910.146/147, OSHA 1926.650-652, and all manufacturer installation manuals.

Class B — Closure: WV Title 33 Series 30, 40 CFR 280, API RP 1604, NIOSH 80-106, OSHA 1910.146, and WV closure guidance documents.

Class C — Tester: WV Title 33 Series 30, PEI RP1200-19, 40 CFR 280, WVDEP tightness test documentation, FE Petro STP MLD Manual, Red Jacket Veeder Root manual.

Class F — Repair Tech: WV Title 33 Series 30, PEI RP100-20, PEI RP1200-19, 40 CFR 280, and manufacturer manuals.
